Historical Aerial Photography — Summer 1948
Survey Year: 1948 Willamette Meridian Scale 1:20,000 Panchromatic Film

A comprehensive aerial photographic survey of Yamhill County, Oregon, conducted in the summer of 1948. Coverage spans Townships 2S through 6S and Ranges 1W through 9W, Willamette Meridian — documenting farmsteads, timberlands, and rural landscapes of the northern Willamette Valley at the moment before post-war development transformed the region.
